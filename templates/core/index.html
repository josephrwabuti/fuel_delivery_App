Platform Features

Everything You Need,
All in One Place

From placing an order to tracking delivery in real-time — FuelGo handles every step.

Find Nearby Stations

Customers discover verified fuel stations closest to their location with live availability and pricing.

Real-Time Delivery Tracking

Track your driver's live location from dispatch to doorstep. Know exactly when your fuel arrives.

AI Demand Prediction

Machine learning models forecast fuel demand by region and time, helping stations stay stocked.

Stock & Price Management

Station owners update fuel stock levels and prices in real-time, keeping customers informed.

Driver Management

Assign verified drivers to orders instantly. Drivers get route details and update status on the go.

Analytics & Reports

System-wide dashboards for administrators, and per-station reports for fuel service providers.

The Workflow

From Order to Tank,
In Minutes

01
Customer Places Order

Choose your fuel type, quantity, and delivery location. Pick a nearby verified station.

02
Station Receives & Assigns

The fuel service provider accepts the order and assigns an available driver for delivery.

03
Driver Delivers Fuel

The driver picks up the fuel and delivers it to your location while you track in real-time.

04
Customer Confirms

You confirm receipt of delivery. Payment is processed and order is marked complete.

05
Data Powers Prediction

Each completed order feeds the AI model, improving demand forecasts for all partners.
